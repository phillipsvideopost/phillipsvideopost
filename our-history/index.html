Our History

After 10 years of fast-paced, high-stakes editing at ABC4 in Salt Lake City, I established Phillips Video Post in 1997.

My hope was to branch out creatively and have the time to devote my all of energies to the work I love. Gratefully, the plan worked. Phillips Video Post has been a success and has provided for my family for more than 20 years.

With 35+ years of producing and editing experience, I offer a unique blend of quality, creativity and speed. Clients have said over and over that their projects are always completed on-time, on-budget, and even better than they had hoped.

I work with a trusted team of professionals who will take your project from idea to storyboard to shoot to final presentation. Making your project a success is our highest priority.

Over the past 20 years Phillips Video Post has stayed on the cutting edge of today’s digital technology. But what hasn’t changed is our commitment to quality production and meeting client deadlines. Every video gets our obsessive attention to detail.

Over the years we’ve noticed that once we earn a client, they stay a client. And often they recommend us to their associates. Please allow us the chance to show what we can do for you.
